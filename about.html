Setting the standard in precision printing

Environmental responsibility in every print

At Digital Press, sustainability is built into how we print, source, and operate. We work closely with Australian and international paper mills that meet or exceed recognised environmental accreditation standards, and we actively embrace responsible printing practices that reduce waste, energy use, and environmental impact—without compromising quality.

Top 5 environmental commitments

  • Digital printing that reduces waste through short, accurate production runs.
  • Minimal make-ready paper compared to traditional offset printing.
  • Fuji Xerox Iridesse press certified by INGEDE for efficient de-inking and recycling.
  • Active recycling of paper and consumables.
  • No volatile organic compounds (VOCs) or petroleum-based inks.

Precision printing with advanced specialty inks

Our flagship Fuji Xerox Iridesse Press combines cutting-edge digital technology with exceptional colour accuracy, enabling consistently high-quality print with premium finishes—produced efficiently in a single pass.

Core Capabilities

  • CMYK plus metallic gold, silver, and Pantone metallic colours.
  • Super-opaque white ink and spot gloss varnish.
  • Up to 6 colours in one pass.
  • Supports stocks up to 400gsm.
  • Sheet sizes up to 330 × 1200mm.

File Supply Spec Sheet

File Format & Delivery

  • High-quality print PDF or packaged InDesign files
  • Supplied as single pages
  • Spot plates exported for Special Inks, UV, and foils

Bleed, Trim & Safety

  • 3mm bleed with crop marks
  • No printer marks or slug lines
  • Minimum 5mm safe margin from trim
  • 10mm clearance from bind edge for perfect and wiro binding

Colour & Variable Data

  • Files set to CMYK
  • Pantone colours supported as Spot Colours
  • Recommended rich black: C30 M30 Y30 K100

Special Dry Ink (FX Technology)

CMYK + Special Colours (Gold · Silver · White · Clear)

  • FX inks must be applied as Spot Colours and named exactly as specified
  • Overlay colours set to Multiply.
  • Refer to the Iridesse guide for image masking and Special Ink setup.

Images, Fonts & Line Work

  • CMYK colour mode, minimum 300dpi
  • Embossing and foil artwork supplied as vector files
  • Fonts embedded or converted to outlines
  • Minimum line weight: 0.25pt

Sheet Size Limits

  • Maximum sheet size: 330 × 1000mm
  • Maximum print area: 320 × 990mm

File delivery

  • Email info@digitalpress.com.au (20Mb or less)
  • Larger files can be uploaded to our website.
