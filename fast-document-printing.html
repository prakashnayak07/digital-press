THE DIGITAL PRESS PROCESS

Our Business Card Production Process

A structured, expert-led workflow designed to deliver consistent quality, premium finishes, and reliable results—every time.

Brand & Use-Case Analysis

We begin by understanding how your business card will be used and what it must communicate.

  • Industry and target audience context
  • Usage intent: networking, retail, corporate, or premium gifting
  • Brand personality alignment: minimal, bold, luxury, or creative

Material & Stock Engineering

Paper choice defines durability, feel, and perceived value.

  • Card thickness options from 300–450gsm
  • Coated vs uncoated stock selection
  • Textured, recycled, or specialty paper options
  • Balancing durability with tactile experience

Finish & Enhancement Selection

Finishes are chosen to enhance visibility, texture, and brand memorability.

  • Matte, gloss, or soft-touch lamination
  • Spot UV for logos or key elements
  • Foil stamping: gold, silver, holographic
  • Embossing or debossing effects
  • Rounded or custom die-cut corners

Pre-Press & Proofing

Before printing, every file is checked for accuracy and consistency.

  • Artwork validation and print setup review
  • Colour accuracy and alignment checks
  • Digital or physical proofing (optional)
  • Final print-readiness approval

Precision Printing & Quality Control

Production is handled using commercial-grade equipment and strict QC standards.

  • High-resolution commercial printing presses
  • Batch consistency and colour monitoring
  • Finish alignment and trimming precision
  • Final quality inspection before dispatch

Delivery & Reorder Scalability

We ensure safe delivery today and easy reorders tomorrow.

  • Secure, protective packaging
  • Australia-wide shipping
  • Approved artwork archived for future reprints
  • Scalable quantities as your business grows
